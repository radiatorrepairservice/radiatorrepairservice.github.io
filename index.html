Welcome!

Caruso Auto & Radiator has been serving the greater DuBois and Weedville area's auto, light truck, and industrial radiator repair needs for 25 years. We understand how vital your vehicle is to your daily life; thus, we ensure your repair will be completed in a timely manner. Even more importantly, owner Terry Caruso has the experience necessary to make the repair last.
